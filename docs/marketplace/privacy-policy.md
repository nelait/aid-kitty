# AID Kitty — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 27, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction

AID Kitty ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered platform for generating MVP plans, project documentation, and providing contextual chat capabilities for software development projects. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service through Microsoft AppSource or directly.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- **Microsoft Account Data**: When you sign in via Microsoft Entra ID, we receive your email address, display name, and Azure AD object identifier. We do not receive or store your Microsoft password.
- **Direct Registration**: If you register directly, we collect your username, email address, and a securely hashed password.

### 2.2 Project Data
- Project titles, descriptions, and requirements you provide
- AI-generated MVP plans, documents, and chat conversations
- Prompt templates and builder sessions you create

### 2.3 API Keys
- Third-party AI provider API keys you optionally store (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek). These are stored encrypted and used only to make API calls on your behalf.

### 2.4 Integration Data
- GitHub Personal Access Tokens (if you choose to connect GitHub)
- OpenHands Cloud API keys (if you choose to connect OpenHands)

### 2.5 Usage Data
- Log data including timestamps, feature usage, and error reports
- No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery**: To provide AI-generated plans, documents, and chat responses
- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and manage your session
- **Subscription Management**: To process and manage your Microsoft Marketplace subscription
- **Service Improvement**: To identify and fix bugs and improve performance

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:
- **AI Providers**: Your project requirements are sent to AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek) to generate content. These providers process data per their own privacy policies.
- **Microsoft**: Subscription lifecycle data is exchanged with Microsoft for marketplace billing.
- **Legal Compliance**: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (12 salt rounds)
- API keys are stored with encryption
- All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- JWT tokens expire after 7 days

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Project data can be deleted by you at any time
- Upon subscription cancellation, data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion
- You may request immediate data deletion by contacting support

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your project data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## 8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):
- Our legal basis for processing is contract performance and legitimate interest
- You may contact us to exercise your data protection rights
- You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

## 9. Children's Privacy

AID Kitty is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
